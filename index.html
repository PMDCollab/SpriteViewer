The PMD Portrait Repository

Welcome to the PMD Portrait Repository! This is a comprehensive resource official portraits from the Pokemon Mystery Dungeon NDS games, and custom portraits made in the same style. They are easily downloaded for use in fan projects, and can be imported to SkyTemple. We aim to provide sprites for every Pokemon of every generation!

When using, you must credit the contributors. They can be found on each Pokemon's page.

How to Colaborate!

Have you made a portrait we're missing? Notice errors in existing portraits? Join us on the SkyTemple discord to speak to contributors and submit your own!

Portrait submissions follow these rules and guidelines:

  • All portraits are 40 by 40 pixels.
  • Emotions must be arranged in this pattern. You can download a template for it here.
  • It is preferred that individual portraits contain 15 colors or less.
  • Submissions must not edit official portraits.
